Jessica - Privacy Policy

Thank you for choosing Jessica as your Discord bot! This privacy policy outlines how your data is collected, used, and protected when using Jessica on Discord.

1. Information We Collect

Jessica Discord Bot may collect the following types of information:

  • User Data: This includes your Discord username, user ID, and any data you provide while interacting with the bot (e.g., commands, messages, or any other information you voluntarily provide).
  • Server Data: Information about the server(s) where the bot is installed, such as server ID, server name, and channel IDs.
  • Logs: Jessica may store logs of interactions to improve bot performance and troubleshoot issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Bot Functionality: To enable and operate the bot's features, commands, and services as intended.
  • Improvements: To improve and update Jessica's features, enhance user experience, and troubleshoot any issues that arise.
  • Security: To monitor and ensure the security and proper functioning of the bot.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, regulation, or legal request, we may disclose necessary information to comply with legal obligations.
  • Service Providers: In case we use third-party services (e.g., hosting services), they may have access to limited data only to the extent necessary to perform their functions.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or to comply with legal obligations. Logs and data may be periodically purged to ensure minimal data retention.

5. Your Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
  • Opt-Out: If you do not wish to share your information with the bot, you may choose to remove Jessica from your server.

6. Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no online service is entirely immune to security risks, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted with the updated effective date. Continued use of the bot after any changes will signify your acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy or your data, please join our Support Server
